    Both are different characters having great affinity. They complement each other's deficiencies.

    Both admire each other and have the flexibility to appreciate the positive aspects of the other.
  • Virgo - calm , cool , analytical and intellectual.
    Constatntly strive for perfection within themselves and others.

    Pisces - gentle , sentimental , spiritual and creative.
    Relaxed and easy going. Highly emotional and sometimes lacks self control.
  • Virgo is emotionally stable and has better control over feelings.

    Pisces is a dreamer and often chase too many dreams in parallel.This often leaves them confused which direction to take.
  • Virgo being over analytical may not be able to appreciate the dreamer in Pisces..this could leave Pisces emotionally drained.

    Pisces can learn to become practical from Virgo.
    Virgo can learn the power of faith from Pisces.
  • Virgo is fascinated by creative and imaginative mind of Pisces.
    Pisces admires Virgo's rational thinking.
