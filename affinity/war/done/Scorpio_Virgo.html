• A complementary relationship which could form a fulfilling union.
    This bond is founded on friendship and loyalty.

    Because of their fundamental differences both signs need to make compromises to each other.
  • Virgo is analytical and methodical.
    Scorpio is intense and emotional.
    Virgo is open and honest.
    Scorpio is concealed and secretive.
  • Scorpio is stubborn and it is very difficult to change their views.
    Virgos are flexible and mutable.
    When there are conflicts, the adamant nature of Scorpios can cause Virgos to compromise. However they maintain a friendly bond and conflicts are rare.
  • Both are critical and sometimes too critical of one another.

    Scorpios do not like to be guided on what to do and never forgets any criticism, but Virgos being perfectionists are likely to be critical.
  • Once the bond is formed and goals have been established nobody can stop this pair from achieving it.

    Virgo likes Scorpio's passion and Scorpio respects Virgo's commitment and rationality. They work together as a team and the combined strength of this team is unbeatable.
