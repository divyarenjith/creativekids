• This combination is not going to be boring.
    Aries - Fire Sign
    Cancer - Water Sign

    Aries - Impatient, adventurous and always looks for new challenges.
    Cancer - Home loving,emotional,patient,stable and always looks for security.
  • You might expect fearless Aries to dominate sensitive Cancer, but..
    Cancerians are very capable of standing up for themselves and wound with words when Aries lets their temper fly.
  • Cancer can teach Aries - how to be stable in life.
    Aries can teach Cancer - to be fast and how to work with emotions.

    Both like to be the boss and get things done..but not in compatible ways.
  • The blend is not likely to be harmonious; but could be highly productive.

    Cancer likes to hold down the fort and Aries likes storms into action - an interesting mix for drama.
