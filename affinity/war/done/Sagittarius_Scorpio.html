• Two strong characters with different nature. This pair need to take time to know each other.

    Sagittarius are open,friendly,social,adventurous and jolly personalities. They are direct,optimistic and always positive.
  • Scorpions are very passionate,loyal,strong,ambitious and self absorbed.

    They look to be calm and friendly to the outer world but could have a very different inner face and emotions deep inside their heart.
  • Sagittarius takes everything casually but Scorpio is very serious about relationships. Both are determined to reach their goals, but through different ways.

    Sagittarius are sometimes blunt and demanding until they get what they want. Scorpions often gets wounded by Sagittarian bluntness, which they will never forget but wait for a perfect opportunity to retaliate.
  • Sagittarius are very open and often do digging to know about others but Scorpions are a bit secretive and won't reveal their mind quickly.

    Strong characters but not compatible , but can still make good friends and colleagues.
